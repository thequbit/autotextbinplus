OLD BUSINESS:
MATTER RE: Update - Brighton Special Events / Park Use Permit - DiPonzio / Catholdi / Keef
Discussion about adopted policies and fees for Brighton Special Events, final draft to be submitted to the Town
Board for approval.
MATTER RE: Changes to Town of Brighton Code - Dogs - Section - 40-14 – Aman
Dan discussed several changes to the Dog section and will be submitting these changes to be reviewed and
possibly adopted by the Town Board.
MATTER RE: Blossom/North Landing Road Intersection/NYSDOT Response – Keef
Letter to the committee stating that a review was completed and would suggest some new warning signage along
roadway as you approach the intersection. Letter is received and filed.
MATTER RE: Highland Avenue Concerns
Chairman DiPonzio addressed recent email notification that an accident took place along Highland Avenue. Chief
Henderson responded that the accident was mainly driving too fast for conditions as the roads where snow covered
and slippery. Chairman encouraged the residents along Highland Avenue contact Tim Keef to express their
concerns as Highland Avenue is due for repaving next year. Some basic reworking of the area may be able to be
put together as the resurfacing is completed.

NEW BUSINESS:
MATTER RE: Children at Play Signs for Meadow Drive, Monroe Parkway and Elwell Drive – Keef
Tim Keef stated that this matter has been reviewed several times and the committee has not granted previous
requests and would be contacting the applicant back to re-enforce the committee decision to not have additional
children at play signage added.

SET TIME AND DATE OF NEXT MEETING:

March 11, 2014 in the Community Meeting Room, Brighton Town Hall.
