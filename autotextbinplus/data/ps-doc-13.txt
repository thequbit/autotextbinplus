OPEN FORUM
Posted weight limit signage has been identified as not enforceable signage and speed along Highland Avenue
have been monitored by officer SMART. Mr. Gerek asked if the Town Board would be interested in adopting a
local law to allow for the enforcement of a posted weight limit. Highland Avenue is a Town of Brighton Road
but the City of Rochester also shares sections along the full length of this highway. Staff to collect some data,
review laws and work with City of Rochester to put together a potential plan.

OLD BUSINESS:
MATTER RE: Proposed No Parking - Chelmsford Road Triangle – Keef / Tuohey
Tim Anderson and Capt. Cline reviewed two (2) locations for NO PARKING posting along this section of road.
Public Works to take this up at next meeting and may bring this to a final conclusion with recommendation to
Town Board.

MATTER RE: Corbett's Glen parking on Glen Road – Keef / DiPonzio
Received some correspondence but no clear consensus for any change. Potential change to parking
ordinance to remove some already adopted language.
MATTER RE: Update Warren Avenue – Traffic Calming Measures – DiPonzio /Keef
Tim Keef stated we were taking a stepped approach to continue to address traffic calming measures with
potential driver and neighborhood feedback about signage, education and enforcement.

MATTER RE: Brighton Special Events Update- DiPonzio / Cline
Brighton High School will be undergoing so renovations during the 4th of July and could impact the events
including the 5K.
Canal Route is the preferred route for future races and Canal Authority will require an application and Town
may adopt a fee schedule to offset set up and associated cost.

MATTER RE: Comprehensive local ordinance for second hand transactions – Henderson
Monroe County will not being enacting this type legislation and we will be reviewing our legislation to see if it
does impact local business and if we will be moving ahead with our local adoption.
MATTER RE: Proposed Speed Limit Reductions Hibiscus Drive, Coral and Crandon Ways – Keef
Town Board passed the speed reduction and signs will be posted as soon as fabricated.
MATTER RE: Potential Contract or Bid for Board up Contractor Services – Roth
This mater will be brought to FASC to be given approval to solicit bids for emergency services for buildings and
structures.
MATTER RE: Fire Hydrant Markings – Park at Allens Creek – Roth / MacAdam
No Report
NEW BUSINESS:
MATTER RE: Woody’s II - 2758 West Henrietta Road – Unsafe Building – Keef

A report was drafted and filed with this committee and forwarded to the Town Board for review and potential
set of a public hearing.

MATTER RE: Pedestrian Crosswalk – Winton Road – Keef
Pedestrian crossing proposed to be relocated to Newton Drive to increase student safety and potentially control

approvals.
