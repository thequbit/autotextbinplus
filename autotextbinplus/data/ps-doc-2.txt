MATTER RE: Baseball Battle – Fireworks Event – Keef
Discussion about activity for charity and fun baseball event (October 28) followed by fireworks at Buckland Park.

MATTER RE: Country Club of Rochester – Private Wedding (Fireworks Request)
Upon review of letter and request the committee recommended that the applicant apply to the Zoning Board of
Appeals for a Temporary and Revocable Permit for their event. If the Temporary and Revocable Permit is granted
than the normal Town of Brighton – Fire Code Permit could be granted. As a footnote, the applicant was advised to
use the same post card notification process so the area neighborhood are aware if the event is granted.
