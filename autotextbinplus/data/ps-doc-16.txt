MATTER RE: Proposed No Parking - Chelmsford Road Triangle – Keef / Tuohey

MATTER RE: Warren Avenue - Speed Humps Request – DiPonzio

out early March.
MATTER RE: Town of Brighton Events / Tour de Cure- DiPonzio / Cline
Angels of Mercy - Have designed a different route and addressed the committee about staying
entirely on the canal path. One route for walker and one route for runners. Proposed to start runners
around 8:00 - 8:30 followed by walkers.
Indian Landing School - Triathlon - Event has encounter some concerns and recently scaled
backed to Bike Route throughout neighborhood and run within the school property. Information
nights proposed to express the rules and understand the program. Penfield School District to offset
the cost of Brighton PD. May 4th is the proposed date of the event and time is flexible to meet
availability of Brighton Police Department (10 AM - NOON). Possible to merge this with Brighton PD
bicycle safety program for outreach and education.
Pink Ribbon Run - East River Road - Kendrick Road- Left a message that they are reviewing their
route and impact to neighborhoods but working with U of R and City of Rochester to find a better plan
to lessen the impact of the event. May 12th (Mothers Day).
Tour de Cure - Captain Cline to meet with MCSD ASAP to review the proposed course
MATTER RE: Comprehensive local ordinance for second hand transactions – Henderson
