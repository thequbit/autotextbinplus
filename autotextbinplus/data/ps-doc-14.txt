OLD BUSINESS:
MATTER RE: Proposed No Parking - Chelmsford Road Triangle – Keef / Tuohey
Questions about placement of signage on existing signage within the grass area. Spacing of signage
to be consistent with posting standards. Staff to review spacing and posting to make these new signs
enforceable.
MATTER RE: Corbett's Glen parking on Glen Road – Keef / DiPonzio
No new development but the parking season is approaching and continue to look for solutions
MATTER RE: Update Warren Avenue - Speed Calming Measures – DiPonzio /Keef
Public Works Committee will be doing a phased approached to improvements. Set public hearing to
reduce the speed limit to 25 mph along streets Hibiscus Drive, Coral and Crandon Ways to have
consistent speed limits within the residential neighborhood.
MATTER RE Brighton Special Events Update- DiPonzio / Cline
An established route along the Erie Canal and Meridian Park to reduce street closure and
neighborhood impact. Discussion about staff with opening facilities but communications could be
established to improve opening the restrooms early for events. An application fee will be established
to cover associated costs.
MATTER RE: Comprehensive local ordinance for second hand transactions – Henderson
Follow up at Monroe County meeting tonight, Chief Henderson recommends that we continue with
our local law to establish local control.
