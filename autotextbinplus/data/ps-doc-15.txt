OLD BUSINESS:
MATTER RE: Proposed No Parking - Chelmsford Road Triangle – Keef / Tuohey
Mr. Tuohey was at the last meeting and this may potentially evolve to a neighborhood improvement
district. Town Board to set future Public Hearing to establish NO PARKING posting along Chelmsford
Road.

MATTER RE: Corbett's Glen parking on Glen Road – Keef / DiPonzio
Tim Keef will do some follow up to any suggestions for proposed parking restrictions.

MATTER RE: Warren Avenue - Speed Humps Request – DiPonzio
We have set a March 19th date to hold a neighborhood informational meeting and will send invites
out early March.

MATTER RE: Town of Brighton Events - DiPonzio / Cline
Discussion about increasing event requests and how to properly respond as well as develop a policy
to define Special Events for the Town of Brighton.
MATTER RE: Comprehensive local ordinance for second hand transactions – Henderson
Businesses have re-sale or second hand transactions, mechanism to report sales or transactions and added
the requirement to photograph items. The proposal does address the "garage sales" so the residents will not
have to comply with this ordinance. Public works to also review for possible conflicts with other sections of the
code. The Town Clerk to discuss some administrative concerns with Chief Henderson and Ken Gordon.

MATTER RE: Traffic enforcement issues on Highland Avenue – DiPonzio
Chairman addressed the residents concern and was asked about consideration of Town of Brighton
to enact the weight limit restriction.
