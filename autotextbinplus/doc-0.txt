TOWN OF HENRIETTA, COUNTY OF MONROE, STATE OF NEW YORK, WAS DULY HELD
AT THE HENRIETTA TOWN HALL, 475 CALKINS ROAD, HENRIETTA, NEW YORK ON

RESOLUTION #9-128/2015
On Motion of
Councilman Breese

Seconded by
Councilman Mulligan

WHEREAS, on or about April 13, 2015, the Town of Henrietta received three (3)
applications (the “Applications”) from Passero Associates on behalf of Mike Spoletta and
Morgan Management, LLC, and
WHEREAS, one application seeks to rezone approximately 9 acres of a 37.56 acre
parcel located on the south side of Calkins Road (County Road 39), approximately 2,000
feet west of the intersection of East Henrietta Road (NYS Route 15A) (tax map
identification #176.05-1-59) (“37-Acre Parcel”) that is currently zoned “Residential” to
“Commercial,” and said Property is currently owned by the Monroe County Fair and
Recreation Association, Inc., and
WHEREAS, a second application from Passero Associates submitted on behalf of
Mike Spoletta and Morgan Management, LLC seeks a Special Use Permit to construct
multi-family housing on 17 acres of land located at the southwest corner of the 37-Acre
Parcel, and
WHEREAS, a third application from Passero Associates submitted on behalf of
Mike Spoletta and Morgan Management, LLC seeks to amend the existing Special Use
Permit previously granted on an adjoining 11.38 acre parcel of property at 2695 East
Henrietta Road on which the Dome Center is currently located (tax map parcel
#176.06-1-74.1) (“11-Acre Parcel”) and said Property is currently owned by the Monroe
County Fair and Recreation Association Inc., and
WHEREAS, after review the Henrietta Town Board has determined that the
Applications are complete, and
WHEREAS, the Town’s Comprehensive Plan also contemplates the construction
of a Library in this area and a Recreation Center that may be constructed on a portion
of the parcel of Town-owned property located at 475 Calkins Road (tax map
identification number 176.05-1-55.11), and
WHEREAS, except in special circumstances, considering only a part, or segment,
of an overall action is contrary to the intent of State Environmental Quality Review Act
(“SEQR”), and

WHEREAS, the Applications and the potential development of the 37-acre parcel
(including the rezoning of an approximate 9-acre portion from Residential to
Commercial, the grant of a Special Use Permit to permit the construction of multi-family
housing and the construction of a library and commercial buildings), the amendment to
the existing Special Use Permit on the 11-acre parcel and the proposed construction of
a Recreation Center at 475 Calkins Road are collectively referred to herein as the
“Action” pursuant to SEQR, and
WHEREAS, the Action is subject to a full and complete environmental review
pursuant to SEQR, and
WHEREAS, to avoid segmentation, the Town Board will review the environmental
impact of the entire Action, and
WHEREAS, Passero Associates has also submitted an Environmental Assessment
Form (“EAF”) to the Town Board, and
WHEREAS, the Action has been preliminarily classified as a Type 1 action
pursuant to SEQR, and
WHEREAS, in accordance with the SEQR regulations, the Town Board wishes to
serve as Lead Agency to conduct a coordinated environmental review of the Action.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that, in accordance with SEQR, the Town Board
of the Town of Henrietta hereby declares its intent to serve as Lead Agency.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Supervisor of the Town be, and hereby is,
authorized to execute the Lead Agency request which shall include an EAF.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Town Supervisor shall cause the Lead Agency
request and EAF to be filed and circulated with the involved agencies designated in the
request, thereby notifying the involved agencies of the Town Board’s intent to serve as
Lead Agency.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that in absence of objections to the Town Board’s
intent to serve as Lead Agency within the thirty day period following notification, the
Town Board of the Town of Henrietta shall proceed to assume Lead Agency status with
respect to the Action.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that Peter C. Minotti, Deputy Supervisor is hereby
directed to forward a copy of this resolution, its attachments and the Application, to the
Town of Henrietta, pursuant to the Henrietta Town Code, Attorneys for the Town and
the Monroe County Planning Board, pursuant to New York State Law.

IN THE MATTER OF
THE ADOPTION OF PROPOSED LOCAL LAW NO. __ OF 2015:
A LOCAL LAW ESTABLISHING A RENTAL REGISTRY
WHEREAS, true and correct copies of proposed Local Law No. __ of 2015, A
Local Law Establishing a Rental Registry, were delivered to each member of the Town
Board, and
WHEREAS, due consideration has been given to the adoption of proposed Local
Law No. __ of 2015 by all members of the Town Board, and
WHEREAS, it was the considered opinion of the Town Board that a Public
Hearing should be held on the 6th day of May, 2015, at the Henrietta Town Hall, 475
Calkins Road, Henrietta, New York, to consider the adoption of the proposed Local Law
No. __ of 2015.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that a Public Hearing be held on the 6th day of
May 2015 at 7:00 p.m., at the Henrietta Town Hall, 475 Calkins Road, Henrietta, New
York, on the question of the adoption of proposed Local Law No. __ of 2015, a Local
Law Establishing a Rental Registry, and a summary thereof be published in a
newspaper previously designated as an official newspaper for publication of public
notices.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Town Clerk shall post Certified Copies of
both this Resolution and said proposed Local Law No. __ of 2015, or a summary
thereof, on the bulletin board maintained by the Town Clerk, pursuant to Section 30(6)
of the Town Law.
